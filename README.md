# Hr Workforce Compensation Analytics Dashboard

**OVERVIEW**
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce composition, compensation trends, hiring activity and employee demogarphics
The project helps HR leaders and business managers understand employee distribution across departments, gender balance, contract types, salary structure, and geographic presence.

**OBJECTIVES**
- Measures total workforce size and salary cost.
- Analyze workforce diversity and gender mix
- Compare compensation across departments
- Track hiring activity over time
- Understand contract type distribution
- Evaluate workforce spread across countries and departments

**KEY INSIGHTS**
- Total workforce consisted of 241 employees.
- Total Salary Cost reached approximately 19M
- Average salary stood at 74k
- 66% of employees were permanent staff, showing a stable workforce structure.
- Department level analysis highlighted differences in headcount and compensation concentration
- Geographic spread shows a multi-loaction workforce including remote employees.

**TOOLS USED**
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
